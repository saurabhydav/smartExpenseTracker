Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Smart Expense Tracker ("we", "us", or "our") handles your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google, we access your basic profile information (specifically your email address and name) to create and identify your account on our servers.
  • Expense Data: All transaction descriptions, amounts, and dates are stored locally on your device in a secure SQLite database. This data is not sent to our servers except when you explicitly choose to backup your data.

2. Google Drive Backup Access

Our app requests access to the Google Drive appDataFolder scope (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata) to allow you to back up and restore your transaction data:

  • This scope only grants our app access to a hidden sandbox folder created specifically for our application. We cannot see, modify, or delete any other files in your personal Google Drive.
  • Your SQLite database file is uploaded directly to this secure sandbox.
  • This backup is completely private and only accessible by you when logged in to our app.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Verify your identity and authenticate your account.
  • Provide the backup and restore functionality.
  • We do not sell, lease, or share your personal information or transaction history with any third parties under any circumstances.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Your local expense data is deleted when you uninstall the app. If you wish to delete your backend account and Google Drive backup files, you can do so by contacting us at the email address below, and we will permanently remove all your records from our servers.
